My Favourite Cartoon Character

TaTa From BT21

I started to fall in love with TaTa as soon as I started to watch the cartoon programme BT21 Here is some info on TaTa:

Appearance
TATA is an alien that has heart-shaped head with yellow lips, and a blue body with yellow polka-dots.

Personality
TATA is from outer space that can perform love with magic. When TATA gets fed up with magic and instead wishes to put effort to show love, they runs away to Earth with VAN. TATA is responsible for the creation of BT21.
